Chickpea Veggie Salad

Home

Description

This refreshing Chickpea Veggie Salad is packed with protein, fiber, and color. It’s made with canned chickpeas, crunchy vegetables, and a tangy lemon-olive oil dressing. It’s great as a light meal or a nutritious side dish.

Ingredients

  • 1 can (15 oz) chickpeas (drained & rinsed)
  • ½ red bell pepper (diced)
  • ½ cucumber (chopped)
  • ¼ red onion (finely chopped)
  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • 1 tbsp lemon juice
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Fresh parsley (chopped, for garnish)

Steps

  1. Combine chickpeas, bell pepper, cucumber, and red onion in a large bowl.
  2. In a small bowl, whisk together olive oil, lemon juice, salt, and pepper.
  3. Pour the dressing over the chickpea mixture and toss gently.
  4. Garnish with chopped parsley and refrigerate for 10–15 minutes before serving.
