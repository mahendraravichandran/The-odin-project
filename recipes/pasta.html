Creamy Garlic Pasta

Home

Description

Creamy Garlic Pasta is a quick and delicious comfort meal made with minimal ingredients. It combines the richness of cream, the punch of garlic, and the heartiness of pasta to deliver a perfect weeknight dinner. The creamy texture and savory flavors make it a hit for garlic lovers.

Ingredients

  • 200g spaghetti or fettuccine
  • 4 cloves garlic (minced)
  • 1 tbsp butter
  • 1 cup heavy cream
  • ½ cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • Salt and black pepper to taste
  • Chopped parsley for garnish

Steps

  1. Cook pasta according to package instructions until al dente. Drain and set aside.
  2. In a large pan, melt butter over medium heat and sauté garlic until fragrant (1–2 mins).
  3. Add cream and bring it to a gentle simmer.
  4. Stir in Parmesan cheese, salt, and pepper. Let it thicken slightly.
  5. Add cooked pasta and toss to coat evenly in the sauce.
  6. Garnish with parsley and serve warm.
